Indoor SLAM with SPOT

SPOT Data Collection:

Indoor data is collected from the SPOT's five stereo vision cameras, a Velodyne LiDAR, and Spot’s internal odometry. The data is collected in multiple sessions in different indoor environments. External Jetson Orion Nano is connected to Spot’s I/O interface for data collection as transfer via WiFi is constrained. Remote management was handled via SSH with custom ROS launch files.

Simultaneous Localization And Mapping (SLAM):

Implemented RTAB-Map SLAM in ROS to generate detailed point clouds, as shown in the videos below. Currently, I am working on ROMAN SLAM to fuse metric and semantic mapping, leveraging scene graph representations for improved environment understanding and object-centric, view-invariant localization on the collected dataset.
