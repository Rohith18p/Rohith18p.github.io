Acrobot Swing up Control

Details

This project is part of my Control Systems course, focuses on achieving swing-up control of an Acrobot using energy-based control and Linear Quadratic Regulator (LQR) in Drake. Through this work, I aim to deepen my understanding of underactuated systems and their stabilization. Project details will be updated as progress continues.

Research Paper: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/350934

Citation: https://gymnasium.farama.org/environments/classic_control/acrobot/
