Acrobot Swing-up Control

The goal of this project was to design a control strategy that enables an Acrobot to perform a swing-up maneuver from the downward stable position to the unstable upright position, and then stabilize at the top. This project demonstrates the integration of energy shaping, optimal control, and switching strategies on a highly non-linear system.


An energy-based controller was implemented to gradually increase the Acrobotâ€™s total energy, bringing it to the upright configuration. Once near the top, control was switched to a Linear Quadratic Regulator (LQR), designed from the linearized dynamics around the equilibrium point, to stabilize the system.
