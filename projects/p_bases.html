Holonomic Mobile Bases

I designed and developed three holonomic bases tailored for different robotic applications: a four-wheeled mecanum drive for smooth omnidirectional motion, a four-wheeled omni drive for enhanced maneuverability and stability, and a compact three-wheeled omni drive optimized for precise control in constrained spaces.


All platforms were powered by encoder motors with PID control, ensuring accurate feedback and highly responsive navigation, operated via joystick or RC controller.
