Bionic Hand

Developed a fully dexterous robotic hand capable of in-hand manipulation and pick-and-place tasks, implementing and testing various control algorithms. The prototype features three fingers at a 1:1.5 scale of the human hand, actuated with Dynamixel motors, along with a custom thumb mechanism enabling abduction and adduction for enhanced dexterity.
