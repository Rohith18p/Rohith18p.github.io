Robocon 2021 - Arrow Throwing Robot

-->

Designed and developed a coordinated dual-robot system — one autonomous and one manual — for the “Arrow Throwing” theme at Robocon 2021. The manual robot featured precise navigation and a robust throwing mechanism, while the autonomous robot integrated sensor-based target detection, trajectory planning, and pneumatic actuation for automated arrow launching.
