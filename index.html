I am a robotics enthusiast driven by the thrill of transforming theoretical concepts into tangible systems that come to life. My passion lies in building robots that harmoniously integrate knowledge from diverse domains, crafting intelligent systems. Over the years, I have honed my expertise in designing and prototyping a wide variety of robots, including manipulators, legged robots, wheeled robots, and drones. My journey has evolved from focusing on robust mechanical designs to creating intelligent, precisely controlled autonomous systems. Inspired by nature, my primary interest lies in developing bio-inspired robots by leveraging the synergy between advanced control strategies and reinforcement learning. With experience in both perception and control, I aim to push the boundaries of robotics, crafting solutions that bridge innovation and functionality.
